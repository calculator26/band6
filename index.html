Study Science
🧠 What Actually Works
Active Recall — Close your notes and write everything you remember. More effective than re-reading.
Spaced Repetition — Review at increasing intervals. Anki works well for vocab, dates, cases and formulas.
Past Papers — The HSC is predictable. Every paper from 2015 onwards under strict timed conditions.
Feynman Technique — Explain any concept in plain language. If you can teach it, you know it.
